Online-Banking Multiplatform Solution

Mission

We developed, tested and maintainaned Online-Banking multiplatform solution for a well-known European bank. The App has a built-in Digipass for Mobile - a two-factor authentication device that allows simple yet secure management of your accounts in a very controlled way.

The use of Digipass for Mobile is not limited to the App - it can be used through any preferred banking channel, e.g. web, phone or fax. The app allows quick and simple, yet fully-secure access to your funds:

  • All types of International and domestic payments in over than 20 currencies
  • FX transactions
  • Access to the account balances and transaction lists
  • Term deposit and loan portfolio overview
  • Credit card transactions and hold amounts
  • ID with any Digipass Token and extra PIN protection
